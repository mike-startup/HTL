The Library Page

The Library page will be a curated collection of books that have been sanctioned and endorsed by Healing The Land Ministries. This page will serve as a resource for spiritual growth, providing visitors with literature that aligns with the ministry's mission and teachings. Primarily dedicated to the late founder, this page will highlight books that reflect the values and vision they instilled in HTL. Each book will be thoughtfully selected to inspire and equip individuals on their spiritual journey, continuing the founder’s legacy of faith and transformation. Through this Library, we aim to offer our community trusted spiritual resources while honoring the founder’s lasting impact on the ministry.
